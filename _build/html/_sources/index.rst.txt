Welcome to POS-Desktop Application's documentation!
===================================================

A Point of Sale (POS) system is a crucial component in retail and hospitality, 
streamlining the sales process in a single integrated solution. It efficiently 
processes transactions by capturing item details, quantities, and prices. 
Simultaneously, POS systems offer robust inventory management, allowing 
businesses to monitor stock levels in real-time, minimizing the risk of stockouts
or excess inventory. Additionally, these systems handle diverse payment methods, 
including cash, credit/debit cards, and digital wallets, providing a seamless 
payment experience. In essence, a POS system serves as a comprehensive tool for 
transaction processing, inventory control, and payment management, enhancing 
overall efficiency in the retail and hospitality sectors.
